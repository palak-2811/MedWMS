Workflow

How MedWMS works

A streamlined four-step process from retailer order to final delivery confirmation.

1

Retailer Places Order

Medicine items, quantities, and a digital signature are submitted online.

2

Wholesaler Reviews

Admin sees the order instantly on the dashboard and updates its status.

3

Invoice Generated

A GST invoice is auto-created with a status watermark and retailer signature.

4

Order is Tracked

Both parties track the shipment's live progress until marked Delivered.
